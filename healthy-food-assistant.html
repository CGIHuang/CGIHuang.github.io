Healthy Food Assistant

Time: 15th Nov 2019

There is a close relationship between diet and health. So, today more and more people are starting to pay attention to how to select and cook food healthily, such as finding a recipe from books, the Internet or watching a video,  paying more attention to how much calories they will obtain every day. So, I want to use AR to help people to develop a good healthy diet and I find the best user environment.

In the first, AR's unique feature is that it can combine virtual visual elements with real-world to express complex information clearly to make people understand easily and quickly. I think the supermarket is the best user sceneï¼Œbecause a decision which food they want to buy always is made at that time.

Healthy Food Assistant - Find Problems

Time: 23th Nov 2019

This week, I went to a supermarket as a consumer to do research and I find two problems. The first is difficult for people to remind the recipes information when they are shopping, which leads to people to fail to keep a good habit of buying healthy food. (If you want to cook healthy food, you  have to make a note to remember ingredients information)

The second problem is that people can not get a clear relationship between food composition information and health.
