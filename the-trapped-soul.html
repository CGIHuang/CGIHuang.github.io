The Trapped Soul - A VR Short Adventure Story

Time: 18th Oct 2019

The inspiration behind the story

Coincidentally, these days I have just finished the Avengers Destruction Chapter. Of course, as a big fan of Marvel, I will never miss any details. In the entire film, the most attractive item of the movie is the infinity gauntlet, so the main inspiration of story comes from one of the infinite gems in Marvel comics. (Soul stone).

The Soul Gem :

Allows the user to steal, control, manipulate, and alter living and dead souls. The Soul Gem is also the gateway to an idyllic pocket universe. At full potential, the Soul Gem grants the user control over all life in the universe.

Why choose a type of story is adventure?

The reason why I select adventure rather than other styles such as romantic, horror, suspense story is that a consideration of time and users' emotion, because the whole experience of time is about one and a half minutes, it needs to create a compact storyline meanwhile the content of the story should avoid too horrible to experience to make sure users have a positive experience. Therefore, adventure story is the best choice, it typically has fast-paced, action-packed plot lines

A Script - The Trapped Soul

Time: 23th Oct 2019

Story Structure

This week, I finished the whole structure of the story. It is a first-person perspective short VR adventure story. The user is asked by an escaped soul to save its friends whose souls are trapped in the soul stone. There are two scenes and three events. In each events, I add some interactions which are very crucial elements to drive the develpoment of the story, which  could build a strong connection between the user and story.

Scene 1: A Dim loft and An Old Photo

In the dim loft, you were sitting front the desk. The faint candlelight illuminated a piece of stone and an old photo (an antiquities discovered during an expedition). Suddenly, an escaped soul jumped out to ask you for help.

 

Scene2: Underground Galaxy

There was a bridge connect to the center of platform in the cave. There was hot lava under the bridge. When you looked up at the top of the cave, you were shocked by the supernatural phenomenon. Countless trapped souls flashed a kind of faint blue light at the top of the cave around the stalactites that had experienced annual erosion for millennia, which looked like an endless galaxy. Suddenly, an escaped soul flew to you and guided you to the depths of the cave.

 

Scene 3: Broke the Soul Stone

When you reached to the center of the cave, there was an artificially carved stone pillar. A fist-sized stone suspended on the platform. The escaped soul seemed to be very restless. It started to try to hit the stone, again and again, to break the stone with its body and asked you for help it to break the stone together.

 

Scene 4: The Angry Guard

Suddenly, the Soul Stone crushed and smashed. Light waves spread out again and again from the center of the stone pillar with strong shock wave. You were popped up far by the power. A huge 4-head monster came out from the under the bridge suddenly. Unfortunately, you couldn’t escape and had to defeat it with your strength.

 

Scene 5: Light of Hope

The Stone of Soul was completely destroyed and countless souls slowly rise from the bottom of the valley. Eventually, you saved these creatures and escaped from the underground cave.
