The Experience Behind The Project

Time: 15th Oct 2019

This is my first VR Project and I want to use VR by combining light and sound elements to make a interactive game to make the user to experience a sense of fear and pressure in a 360-degree virtual space. The first step, I did a research from the Internet to find some scnes in the real world and I watched a documentary about geographic exploration.

A faint light, eyesight limitation and echo from footsteps and rocks are the best visual and auditory elements for my project.

The Game Mechanism: Light & Echo

Time: 22th Oct 2019

The user will be placed a maze composed by caves. In each cave is connected by three tunnels and each tunnel connects another cave. In order to find the final exit, users need to throw stones picked up from the ground to each tunnel and get feedback of echo. If the length of the echo is the longest from a tunnel, then it should be selected by users. Who across the number of caves is less and he or she will be a winner.

Important Interactive Items: Torch & Stones

Time: 30th Oct 2019

This week, I designed to add two mechanisms to improve the realism of interaction. One was the strength of fire would become weaker gradually, which made the user's eyesight smaller and smaller. So, users had to pick up stones and throw to them quickly before they couldn't see anything. Another was the limited number of stones in each cave, which forced users to listen to the echo carefully. Both elements were the common sense of nature in the real world.
